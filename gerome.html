GEREME

Located among the "fairy chimney" rock formations.
A town in Cappadocia, a historical region of Turkey.
Has a population of around 2,000 people.
An important tourist destination,
a "center" for all tourism in Cappadocia.
Historically important sites are:
Ortahane, Durmus Kadir, Yusuf Koc.

HOT BALLOON

Gerome is the most popular spots to go up in a Hot Balloon.
As you take off for sunrise you will be hypnotised
by the spectacular and surreal landscape that lies below you.

ATV

Explore the off-the-beaten-paths on an ATV.
An exciting adventure over and through
the beautiful hills, valleys, dunes and trails of
the extraordinary Cappadocia landscape.
From Goreme to Rose Valley and Love Valley.
