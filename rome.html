ROME

the capital of Italy and of the Lazio region.
It is also the country's largest and fourth-most
populous city in the European Union.
Rome's history spans more than
two and a half thousand years.
The city's early population originated
from a mix of Latins, Etruscans and Sabines.

COLOSSEUM

In the centre of the city of Rome.
Built of concrete and sand.
It is the largest amphitheatre ever built.
Construction began under the emperor Vespasian in AD 72
and was completed in AD 80 under his successor.

VITTORIANO MONUMENT

Built in honour of the first king Victor.
Between the Piazza Venezia & the Capitoline Hill.
The eclectic structure was designed by Sacconi.
Sculpture was parceled out to established sculptors.
Inaugurated in 1911 and completed in 1925.
